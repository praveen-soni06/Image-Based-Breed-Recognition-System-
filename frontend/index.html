Popular Breeds of India

Explore the diversity of Indian cattle and buffalo breeds — each card contains short notes on traits and origin.

Gir

Region: Gujarat · Trait: High milk yield & disease resistance.

Murrah

Region: Haryana/Punjab · Trait: High-fat milk, very popular buffalo breed.

Sahiwal

Region: Punjab · Trait: High milk production, calm temperament.

Kankrej

Region: Gujarat/Rajasthan · Trait: Dual-purpose (milk & draught).

Tharparkar

Region: Rajasthan · Trait: Drought tolerant, hardy dairy breed.

Mehsana

Region: Gujarat · Trait: High butterfat content in milk.

Red Sindhi

Region: Sindh · Trait: Fertility & adaptability in tropical climates.

Ongole

Region: Andhra Pradesh · Trait: Strong draught breed, disease resistant.
